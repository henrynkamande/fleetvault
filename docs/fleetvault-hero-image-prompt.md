# FleetVault hero image — ChatGPT / DALL·E prompt

Copy everything below the line into ChatGPT (with image generation enabled) or another image model.

---

Create a **wide landscape hero image** (16:9 aspect ratio, 1920×1080 or similar) for a B2B SaaS marketing website called **FleetVault**.

**Brand & mood**
- Modern, trustworthy, professional — not cartoonish, not stock-photo cliché (no handshake, no pointing at laptop).
- Conveys **fleet operations, logistics, and control** without showing GPS tracking maps, surveillance, or “dots on a map” tracking drivers.
- Privacy-first, human-respectful tone: confident operations team, not dystopian tech.

**Visual subject**
- A **fleet yard or urban logistics scene**: 2–4 commercial vehicles (vans or light trucks) in clean condition, optional warehouse or depot in soft background.
- Optional: fleet manager or owner in business-casual attire reviewing a **tablet or laptop** with abstract dashboard UI (blurred, no readable text).
- Golden hour or soft daylight; subtle depth of field; cinematic but realistic photography style.

**Color palette (match website)**
- Primary accent: **deep blue** `#2f5aab`
- Secondary accent: **amber/gold** `#fbbd26`
- Neutrals: slate gray, off-white sky, dark navy `#0f172a` in shadows
- Overall: cool blues + warm amber highlights, not overly saturated

**Composition**
- Leave **negative space on the left or center-left** for marketing headline overlay (darker gradient-friendly area).
- Subject weight toward the **right third** of the frame.
- No logos, no watermarks, no text in the image.

**Avoid**
- Live GPS maps, red tracking pins, “big brother” eye motifs
- Aggressive military or police imagery
- Crowded highways with accidents
- Low resolution, AI artifacts on hands/faces, extra fingers

**Output**
- Photorealistic or high-end commercial photography look
- Suitable as website hero background with dark gradient overlay and white headline text on top

**Optional refinement follow-up** (paste after first result if needed):

> Make the left 40% of the image slightly darker and less busy for text overlay. Reduce saturation slightly. Ensure vehicles look like African or international light commercial fleets (Toyota Hiace–style vans acceptable). No readable UI text on screens.

---

**After you generate the image**
1. Save as `fleet-hero.png` (or WebP for performance).
2. Place in `frontend/public/fleet-hero.png` (replacing the current file).
3. Hard-refresh the homepage to verify the hero panel.
